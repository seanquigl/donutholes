Bird-dog of the century

On the day the sun exploded we were all underneath a big white tent
in the golden field where they give out the award
for the bird-dog of the century.
It was a brilliantine day,
a softiness day,
a rexentide day.

Sadie, of course, looked magnificent barking.
And you were looking great in your shorts.
It was a crystalpuff day,
a cinnahot day,
a respiffient day.

We heard singing from the distant rooftops where we bought our bread at.
We were standing in the field under the tent waiting for Sadie to receive her award
for being the bird-dog of the century.
You told Sadie she was a good dog.
It was true.
It was a wispious day,
a bummykins day,
a flyerrod, sizzlehawk, schrumpadump day.
