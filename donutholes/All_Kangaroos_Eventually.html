All Kangaroos Eventually

Created:8/19/2022 3:25 PM
Updated:2/1/2023 9:58 PM
Author:Sean Quigley
Location:33°47'33 N  84°22'38 W

Dry percolator

Hot garbage, the joy
of yesterday’s jigginess while we

dance around

Thief at the parade!
Grand bivouac

Ripped halo destruction

Don’t let me catch you breathing
up a storm
on your beer canoe

Black porches,
The free lithium core

Disheartenment blow...
Frog-hearted chessmen!
Big bowls of ice cream!

Destroyer does Sinatra at the Sands

Seeing you stride through
the meadow you made,

A photo of the thimble court
would dwindle yet

No tall glasses teeter
the rock-bottomed toast...
Stillness!
A remark you made!
Mom taking a nap!

I cry every time I think about the twin towers

Somewhere delicious
A vase of peregrine
falcons fall

and you are
in a gas station bathroom in the early 2000s

Hayrides tend to dwell
too swimmingly!
Shock narrative!

Lossless depression algorithm

My milkshake sends my boys swimming
tobacco downs

Off five Kentucky freeways

Waxen glimpse!
Shepherd’s pie!
Barnyard kisses!

It’s hard not to hate yourself

Often is a sometimes winning
pitcher on a winning team

However champs get wet
they ding a crossfed tuck,
imagine soft pirouettes

Yogis blast
Bread factories!
Girls in big trucks!

UFO sighting above Ruby Cinemas in Franklin, NC

Gallant rainbows prick

wild horses

Incandescence!

Nostalgia emperor

Perfection is a blue Dodge Ram
at any speed

Or backwards...
Poreless crystal text

Car crash!
Shattered rearview!
I am still trying
to get out!

At Robert Pollard’s Tomb

Flutter pizza parlor flag

Closed empire colors fade

Doomed communications!
Sad smiles bloom in darkness!

I will stop smoking when you stop pissing me off

A world demands drowning
So you cancel rather
What clarity gives

Withhold, withhold or
Give give give...?

Hopelessness!
A tiny light in the dark!
Long train cars wobble!

On the day of your wedding

You come to me,
Big piles of gravel

Kindness, rainshowers
Your silhouette

A sailboat

Here come the tough guys

Wack rebellions, nightjoys

‘Get fucked!’
A night crying

twigginess

Obscure divinations

‘How ebullient!’
four...

Five

Dogs barking!

To all my haters

River walks...
These

ARE

confessional poems!

Loose translation of a Song Dynasty love poem

Refrigeration max,
cloud cover

Surf wax pop rocket

All big dogs go
bye
Bye!

I fell asleep in the garden of democratic illusions and woke up a dictator of my own hollow world

Squat machinations carve
a dark
figurine...

Smacked balls bang
The open park’s pergola tops!

Power lines!
Launch angles!

Godzilla teatime

I sing
shovel

black leaves,
Romance

Cities smolder!
Cops unresponsive!
Worlds hover
twinkling!

Fish menagerie

Twisted
flesh

Milk aquarium, motherhood

Product managers demand
Eternal brunches!
Exposed brick!

A podcast of swans

Sick microphones finagle

Attempted ape escape

Wild trumpets!
Folded wings!

The adrenaline bastard

Being beefy is a popsicle lick
Poodle party

Distortions
as badass
as being cool

Xbox afternoons!
Much-loved guns!
Infinite dap-ups!

Airborne crudité reflection

Your personal sandwich garden provokes
Mere egotism
chevron sparks...

Angels twitter
in the Dulles crown room!

To call this ‘feather ballast’—
Too easy!

Not on my clocktower

Hellishness clover
cathedral,
A glance from fringe

Sweet as 7/11
at midnight basketball practice

Personal fantasy cinema!
Tenuous connections!
Brain-light!


Don’t ever stop asking me about my pet parakeet

All kangaroos eventually

Madness,
repetition hop
A barely discernible twist...

Punks in the coffeehouse!
Pope's reverie!

The tallest mountain in Vermont

Your dad keeps yammering

with the heaters off
Intellectual

skeleton inquisition!
Ten thousand trombones!

Mass butterfly grave behind the barbershop

Cumulus
panther dragoon

Marble statue cuddle culminates
lobotomy...

Emotional détente
at the coin museum!
Loving hair strokes!

Mystery dictionary

Cold water,
Why not move
back home?

Cherubim vacillate
incantations

Snowy mornings by the frog pond!
Frogsongs!

More sentimental trash

Skyscrapers on trial,
the porch lamp

Foxes spotlit in the prison yard...

Having a laugh with you!
Shoplifting a Snickers bar!

Accurate representation of an actual life memory

Dishrags,
Crumpled atlas

driving in circles

Thirty-three scented candles!
Breakdown at the Genius Bar!

Aluminum trackstar

Sheer windiness and
the ruffled mark of cliffs

You cut your hair
short equations...

Mad familiarity!

End zone cocktail bucket

Pills stack a trout
wasted Winnebago

Flipper, flip!

Boinged out in the arching boneyard!
Slipperiness!

Brief history of dance music

In the toolshed tango

Batters bat,
We share a food future...

Jackbootlegged twister
Caught in the money gap!
A viscount’s merriness!

Come on down to the park

Crooked web, hot
possibilities

Ball breaks to black

if not in off the plate!
O vertigo!
Hammer curve!

Dubious excuse

We swung
from chandeliers

I promise to make sense every time

No footholds!
No remorse!

Painting with wolves

Colossal perks drip
hugely

All bellweathers swing

Starward!
My river fucks!

Sean Quigley is the author of over two volumes of poems, including On the Wings of 10,000 Eagles, Alien Visitations, and Straßenschmutz. You can read them on donutholes.com . He grew up in Atlanta and is researching and writing about the mimeograph revolution at the University of Vienna.
